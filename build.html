CONSTRUCTION PROCESS

1. Pre-construction Package
While architectural drawings show you what’s being built, our pre-construction package shows you how it needs to happen. Getting accurate quotes for a building project is like comparing apples and oranges without details. So we go much further than providing a basic structural pack. Our pre-construction package consolidates architectural designs into structural calculations and drawings, building regulations specifications, and a detailed project blueprint. It’s your assurance of continuity between initial concept and structural integrity, especially regarding statutory building regulations. We even clarify everything from flooring to light fittings and plumbing points. Nothing is left to interpretation.
2. Costing
Are you interested in Happy Building managing your construction? Or leading a tender process on your behalf? HB BUILT is where we take responsibility for the project cost and completion by acting as the principal contractor. First, we put the best construction team together based on your approval of a meticulously detailed budget. Then we directly manage the building programme for you. Alternatively, HB CONNECT is ideal for clients who are comfortable handling more responsibility. In this case, we’ll connect you with vetted building contractors and support you through the tendering process. Each tender you receive is based on our comprehensive project brief to avoid misinterpretation of the requirements.
3. Build
Things get really exciting if you choose us to manage the construction. Clients love the fact we do the heavy lifting, so they don't have to worry. Everything comes together in harmony as we provide consistency between the design concept and the finished project. Throughout the process, we help you manage the essential choices. The fit and finish include the textures, colours, and second-fix electrics to lighting diagrams, plumbing positions, kitchen and bathroom layouts. This is where your vision comes together in the things you can physically see, feel and touch. It's the culmination of a process that starts with ideas and inspiration and ends with a lifestyle transformation.
It's the Happy Building way.
